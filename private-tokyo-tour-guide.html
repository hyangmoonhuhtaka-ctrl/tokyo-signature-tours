Private Tokyo Tour Guide FAQ

What is a private Tokyo tour guide?

A private Tokyo tour guide is a dedicated local guide for your group only. The guide helps shape the route around your interests, explains cultural context, supports navigation, and keeps the day flexible.

Who is Tokyo Signature Tours best for?

Tokyo Signature Tours is best for couples, families, first-time visitors, and discerning travelers who want a private, relaxed, and thoughtfully planned Tokyo experience instead of a standard group tour.

Can a private Tokyo tour be customized?

Yes. The itinerary can be customized around food, culture, temples, shopping, art, design, history, family-friendly stops, hidden neighborhoods, and your preferred pace.

How is a private Tokyo tour different from a group tour?

A private tour is designed around your group only. There are no flags, microphones, fixed group routes, or pressure to keep pace with strangers. The guide can adjust timing, stops, breaks, and route choices throughout the day.

Can the tour start at our hotel?

In most cases, yes. A private Tokyo tour can start from your hotel lobby or another convenient meeting point in central Tokyo, depending on the route and guide availability.

Which tour length should we choose?

Choose 4 hours for a refined introduction, 6 hours for neighborhoods plus food and culture, and 8 hours for a full signature day covering iconic sights and hidden local areas at a relaxed pace.
