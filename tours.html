8 hours · signature

Full Day Signature Tour

A complete Tokyo day — temples, markets, viewpoints, and hidden corners, woven into one seamless day at your preferred pace.

From $225 per person ($449 for 1 guest · $559 total for 2)

Ideal for

Couples, families, or small parties who want to make the most of one full day in Tokyo with a private guide and a thoughtfully designed plan.

Typical timing

Usually 9:00–17:00 — flexible. Before the tour, you receive a draft itinerary based on your interests, which can still be adjusted on the day.

Possible inclusions

Asakusa & Senso-ji · Tsukiji or Toyosu market · Meiji Shrine & Harajuku · Shibuya Crossing & Shibuya Sky · Imperial Palace · evening Shinjuku or Ginza.

Highlights

  • Tokyo's temples, markets, and neighbourhoods, seen with a knowledgeable local guide
  • Quiet backstreets, artisan shops, and corners away from the crowds
  • Genuine cultural moments and conversations with locals
  • A fully tailored route, flexible start times, and a pre-tour questionnaire

What is included

  • A private, tailored walking tour with a carefully selected bilingual guide
  • Flexible start time, decided together based on your schedule
  • Pre-tour consultation and a short questionnaire to design your itinerary
  • Direct communication via WhatsApp or email before and after the tour

Not included

  • Food and drinks (including meals during the 6-hour food tour — paid directly by guests)
  • Tickets to attractions (temples, museums, observatories, and so on)
  • Transportation costs (walking tour; if we use public transport, guests pay their own fares)
  • Gratuities — appreciated, never expected
