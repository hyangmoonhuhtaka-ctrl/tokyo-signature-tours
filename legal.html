Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Timing and Refund Amount (Japan Time)

Cancellation up to 72 hours before the scheduled tour start time:
→ 100% refund of the tour fee

Cancellation between 24 and 72 hours before the scheduled tour start time:
→ 50% refund of the tour fee

Cancellation less than 24 hours before the scheduled tour start time, late arrival on the day of the tour, or no-show:
→ No refund

All times are calculated based on Japan Standard Time (JST).

2. Refund Method

Refunds are made only to the same credit card used for the original payment, via Stripe.

Refunds cannot be made in cash, by bank transfer, or to a different credit card.

Stripe processing fees, foreign exchange losses, overseas card fees, and similar actual costs may be deducted from the refund amount.

3. Timing of Refund Processing

Where a refund is due, the Company will process the refund within fourteen (14) business days from the date on which the decision to refund is made.

Depending on the card issuer’s processing, it may take approximately 3 to 30 days after processing for the refund to appear on the Customer’s statement.

4. Basis for Calculating Refund Amounts

If any of the following costs arise in connection with the refund, they may be deducted from the refund amount:

  • Stripe payment processing fees
  • Foreign exchange losses
  • Overseas card handling fees

The Company does not compensate for any loss caused by exchange rate fluctuations.

5. Items Not Subject to Refund

The following items are not eligible for refund:

  • Guide’s transportation costs (variable costs incurred on the day)
  • Overtime / tour extension charges
  • Food and beverage costs, transportation costs, admission fees, and activity fees
  • Cancellation fees charged by third-party service providers
  • Gratuities (tips)

Cancellation Due to Force Majeure

If it becomes difficult to conduct the Tour, in whole or in part, due to earthquakes, typhoons, epidemics, public events, transportation disruptions, facility closures, or other circumstances beyond the Company’s reasonable control, the Company may cancel or discontinue the Tour.

Cancellation before the Tour starts
→ 100% refund of the tour fee
(Refund processing: within fourteen (14) business days from the date on which the cancellation decision is made.)

Interruption after the Tour has started
→ The Company will decide, in its reasonable discretion, whether a refund (and in what amount) is appropriate, taking into account the portion of the Services that has not been provided and the costs already incurred.
(Refund processing: within fourteen (14) business days from the date on which the refund decision is made.)

In all cases, airfare, lodging expenses, transportation expenses, and other incidental costs are not covered or reimbursed.
