Our story

A Private, Concierge-Style Experience in Tokyo

Tokyo Signature Tours was founded by Taka, a Tokyo native and former TV director with over 30 years of broadcasting experience and travel across the globe. The mission is simple: to offer travelers private, premium, fully customizable Tokyo experiences led by carefully selected bilingual local guides.

How we work

Concierge-Style Planning, Effortless Days

1 · We listen first

Before your tour, we ask a few questions about your interests, pace, dietary preferences, hotel area, and what you hope to feel — not just see — in Tokyo.

2 · We draft your itinerary

You receive a tailored draft plan in advance. Everything — route, timing, food stops, photo moments — is shaped around you.

3 · We meet, refine, enjoy

On the day, we adapt to your energy and curiosity. Slow down, change course, linger — the day is yours.
