Private Tours in Tokyo

Tokyo Signature Tours offers more than standard sightseeing. Each experience is hand-crafted by Taka, an experienced bilingual local guide, to balance comfort, authenticity, and a touch of elegance.

Before your visit, we take time to understand your preferences — from food and neighborhoods to pace and interests — so that your first days in Tokyo feel smooth, inspiring, and deeply memorable.

Signature Tokyo Experiences

Half Day Tokyo Highlights (4h)

A refined introduction to Tokyo, perfect for your first day or a short stay. We focus on 2–3 key areas so you can quickly feel oriented and at ease.
$180 / person  ($360 for 2 guests)

Ideal for

First-time visitors, couples, or families who want a gentle start in Tokyo, with time to learn local customs and practical tips for the rest of their trip.

Typical Timing

9:00–13:00 or 13:00–17:00 (flexible, adjusted to your schedule).

Extended Half Day & Food Tour (6h)

A deeper exploration of neighborhoods and food culture. We combine essential sights with local backstreets and carefully chosen eateries.
$270 / person  ($540 for 2 guests)

Ideal for

Curious travelers and food lovers who wish to go beyond the highlights and enjoy authentic ramen, sushi, or small neighborhood restaurants.

Typical Timing

Around 10:00–16:00 with one or two relaxed meal stops along the way.

Full Day Signature Tour (8h)

A complete Tokyo day – temples, markets, viewpoints, and hidden gems, woven into one seamless experience at your preferred pace.
$330 / person  ($660 for 2 guests)

Ideal for

Couples, families, or small groups who want to make the most of one full day in Tokyo with a private guide and a thoughtfully designed plan.

Typical Timing

Usually 9:00–17:00 (flexible). Before the tour, you receive a draft itinerary based on your interests, which we can still adjust together on the day.

Highlights

  • Discover Tokyo's temples, markets, and neighborhoods with an expert local guide
  • Uncover quiet backstreets, artisan shops, and off-the-beaten-path corners
  • Engage with locals and experience authentic cultural moments
  • Enjoy a fully tailored route with flexible start times and a pre-tour questionnaire

What's Included

  • Private, customized walking tour with Taka (bilingual Japanese–English guide)
  • Flexible start time decided together based on your schedule and preferences
  • Pre-tour consultation and a short questionnaire to design your itinerary
  • Direct communication via WhatsApp or email before and after the tour

Not Included

  • Food and drinks (including meals during the 6h Food Tour — guests pay directly)
  • Tickets to attractions (temples, museums, observatories, etc.)
  • Transportation costs (this is a walking tour; if we use public transport, you pay your own fares)
  • Gratuities (optional, always appreciated but never expected)
