Frequently Asked Questions About Private Tokyo Tours

How far in advance should we book a private Tokyo tour?

We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during cherry blossom season, autumn, and holidays. This allows us to design a customized itinerary and match you with the most suitable bilingual local guide.

Can you customize a private Tokyo tour?

Yes. Every Tokyo Signature Tour is customized to your interests, pace, and travel style, creating a unique and personal experience in Tokyo.

Do you provide English-speaking guides in Tokyo?

Yes. Our carefully selected bilingual local guides provide cultural insights, local recommendations, and seamless support throughout your private Tokyo tour.

Are private Tokyo tours suitable for children or older guests?

Absolutely. We can adjust the itinerary, walking distance, pace, and transportation to accommodate families with children, seniors, and guests with mobility considerations.

What is included in the private tour fee?

The fee includes private guiding, itinerary planning, bilingual language support, and local navigation. Transportation, meals, drinks, and attraction tickets are paid separately by guests.

What happens if it rains?

Tours usually operate in light rain. If necessary, we adjust the itinerary to include more indoor locations and comfortable alternatives while maintaining the overall experience.
